Our assumptions about how the site search/navigation works

Everything is driven off of taxonomies

Taxonomies

  • Products (multiple)
  • UserTypes (Studio User | Player Developer | API Developer | SDK Developer) (multiple)
  • ProductAreas (single)
  • ProductFoci (single)
  • Keywords (multiple)

https://learning.brightcove.com/video-cloud/managing-videos/managing-assets/adding-captions.html
