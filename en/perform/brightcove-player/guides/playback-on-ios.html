Video Playback on iOS Devices

In this topic, you will learn about playing video with the Brightcove player on iOS devices.

As of iOS 10, there are new <video> policies which include:

  • Inline playback
  • Muted autoplay

For details about these policies, see the New <video> Policies for iOS document.

Inline playback

On the iPhone, you can now use the playsinline attribute to play inline video without automatically entering fullscreen mode when playback begins.

Without the playsinline attribute, you video will continue to require fullscreen mode for playback on the iPhone.

Example: 

<video playsinline>

Muted autoplay

As of iOS 10, autoplay is supported if the source media contains no audio tracks or the video is muted.

With the autoplay attribute, your video will begin playing only when the player is visible on screen, like when it is scrolled into the viewport. Playback will pause when the player is not visible, like when it is scrolled out of the viewport.

Example: 

<video muted autoplay>

Samples

Below are samples using either the Standard (iframe) or Advanced (in-page) embed code.

You can also use Studio to set your Brightcove player to autoplay.

Standard (iframe) embed

With the Standard (iframe) implementation, you can use query parameters to play a video without entering into fullscreen mode and autoplay while muted: 

<iframe
src="//players.brightcove.net/1752604059001/default_default/index.html?videoId=5141730843001&playsinline=true&muted=true&autoplay=true"
allowfullscreen
webkitallowfullscreen
mozallowfullscreen
height="360"
width="640"></iframe>

Advanced (in-page) embed

With the Advanced (in-page) implementation, you can use the attributes on the video element to play a video without entering into fullscreen mode and autoplay while muted: 

<video data-video-id="5141730843001"
data-account="1752604059001"
data-player="default"
data-embed="default"
data-application-id
class="video-js"
controls
height="360"
width="640"
playsinline autoplay muted>
</video>
<script src="//players.brightcove.net/1752604059001/default_default/index.min.js"></script>
