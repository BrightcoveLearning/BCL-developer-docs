What are logical categories for your media assets?

For an effective presentation of your video assets you need to consider logical categories for those assets. The driving force behind the categories should be can your customers find the videos they want logically and quickly.

Types of categories

You have two types of categories to choose from, they are:

  • Those that you build. Under the hood, the categories you build are actually playlists built in Video Cloud Studio.
  • Those that Brightcove Beacon automatically creates, those being Recently Watch, Most Watched and Favorites. Further details are provided in the next section of this document.

College Athletics

It might be that the large quantity of categories at the top level. For instance, there are 347 Division 1 (generally large) schools. You could have the home screen of your app list all 347 schools, but some customers would most likely find that long list too scroll through a poor user experience. You can use sub-categories, or even sub-categories of sub-categories to make the search for a particular set of videos easier. The following displays a partial structure of possible categories for a college athletics site. Here the structure follows the Conference -> School -> Sport organizational structure:

If you believe the sport, rather than the conference, is most important to your customers you would maybe change the categorization to follow the Sport -> Conference -> School organizational structure.

Your Categories

From the examples you can see deciding on your categories and organization structure might be a straightforward, or very complex, task. Take time now to think through and document your organization structure and/or categories.

What Brightcove Beacon built categories do you want?

Brightcove Beacon can provide three categories you can choose to include or not. They are:

  • Recently Watch -
  • Most Watched -
  • Favorites - This category is dynamically built as customers mark video assets as a favorite

In what order should the categories be placed?

Now that you have determined the categories you want for you app, you need to decide in what order they need to appear. Using your work from the last two sections, document here the order the categories should in your app.
