Overview

A Brightcove player plugin that reports player events to the Omniture App Measurement library. For details on how App Measurement works and how to properly configure it, see the App Measurement for iOS Implementation Guide.

This component automatically handles basic video tracking when a user is first presented with a video, when they play or pause a video and when a video concludes. Put in App Measurement terminology, the component will call open, play, stop and close respectively during video playback.

In SiteCatalyst v14, you will need to enable video tracking on the Report Suite you intend to collect data with. To do so, have your account administrator go to Admin > Report Suites from the SiteCatalyst top menu and select the Report Suite you will be using. Then, click Edit Settings > Video Management > Video Tracking Settings. From the Video Tracking settings page, you will need to check “Enable Video Reports” at minimum. For more information on video tracking, see the SiteCatalyst and SiteCatalyst Admin Console documentation.
