Brightcove Player Accessibility

Brightcove Player has numerous accessibility features, detailed below.

Keyboard controls

Brightcove Player can be controlled from the keyboard. The tab order to controls, and keys to manipulate the controls, are as follows:

  1. Big play button: Press Space or Enter to play.
  2. Play button in control bar: Press Space or Enter to toggle between play and pause.
  3. Progress bar: Use up or right buttons to jump forward 5 seconds, use down or left buttons to jump back 5 seconds.
  4. Full screen: Press Space or Enter to toggle between full screen and not full screen.
  5. Volume slider: Use up or right buttons to increase volume, use down or left buttons to decrease volume.

Screen readers

Brightcove player is compatible with screen readers on desktop browsers, as well as iOS and Android devices.
