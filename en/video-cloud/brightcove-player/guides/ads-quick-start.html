Quick Start to Using Ads

This document provides a hands-on introduction to using the IMA3 Plugin to display advertisements in Brightcove Player.

Verify VAST tag

  1. Obtain an ad tag. (If you do not have one, you will be able to copy a demo ad tag URL soon.) In this quick start it is assumed you have an ad that includes a pre-roll, but any inline ad will work.
  2. To verify the validity of your ad tag, be sure you have copied its URL and browse to the following page:

    Video Suite Inspector (clicking this link will cause the page to open in a new window or tab)

  3. Paste your ad tag URL into the Input type form input field. If you do not have an ad tag URL, click the Sample link and one will be provided. Also copy the URL for later use.
  4. Click Test Ad and you will see your ad play followed by the Google supplied motorcycle race video.
  5. To verify your ad tag is compatible with Brightcove Player and its IMA Plugin, browse to the following page:

    Brightcove Player with Advertisements (clicking this link will cause the page to open in a new window or tab)

  6. Paste your ad tag URL into the Ad Server URL form input field.
  7. Click Update.
  8. Play the video and you and you will see your ad play followed by the Brightcove supplied bird video.
  9. Be sure both of these tests complete successfully, then proceed to the next section. If either of these tests failed, you need to check your ad tag.
