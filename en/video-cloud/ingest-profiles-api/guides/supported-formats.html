Supported Video Formats

What incoming video formats are compatible with Dynamic Ingest? Nearly everything! We know that it’s difficult to predict what types of formats might be uploaded to your application, especially with user-generated content, so our goal is to accept every video format possible.

Not supported

It’s actually probably more helpful to talk about the formats we don’t support, since that’s a manageable list. At the moment, there are only a few:

  • Apple Intermediate
  • HDV 720p60
  • Go2Meeting3 (G2M3)
  • Avid Meridien Uncompressed
