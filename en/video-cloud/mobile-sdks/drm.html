Content Security (DRM) with the Native Player SDKs

Digital rights management (DRM) protects your video content by encrypting the video data and unlocking it based on license policies.

The Brightcove Native Player SDK platforms support the following DRM solutions:

Platform DRM Solution
Native Player SDK for iOS Apple's FairPlay Streaming
Native Player SDK for tvOS Apple's FairPlay Streaming
Native Player SDK for Android Google's Widevine Modular

Note: the use of Widevine Classic has been deprecated. We encourage you to migrate to FairPlay Streaming.

Video Cloud customers

Follow these steps to create DRM-enabled content:

  1. Contact your account manager to have your account(s) DRM-enabled.

    Note: to use DRM encryption, your account must be enabled for these features. Contact your account manager for more information.

  2. Configure your account with the proper licensing keys and create custom ingest profiles which define packages for each DRM type (ie. Widevine Modular, FairPlay, etc.)
  3. Use Studio or Dynamic Ingest to create DRM-enabled content based on your custom ingest profiles.
  4. For existing video content in Video Cloud, you can re-transcode using your custom ingest profiles, as long as you initially had used the new Studio Upload module or Dynamic Ingest.
