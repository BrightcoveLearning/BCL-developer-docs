Policy Keys

This topic will discuss the policy keys for your account.

Introduction

When you create a Brightcove player, a policy key is automatically generated for each player. This key is associated with your Brightcove account and controls which videos can be viewed and when they can be viewed.

The policy key is only used to control videos that are stored in Video Cloud.

Getting the policy key

The policy key is automatically generated and managed for you. So, in most cases you will not need to request it or manage it. The easiest way to retrieve your policy key is to do the following:

  1. Create a Brightcove Player in Video CloudPerform Studio.
  2. Click on the URL link (either Preview or Published will work).
  3. In the browser address bar, change the index.html to config.json, and browse the new URL.
  4. In the displayed player configuration you will see the policy key as follows:

In rare instances you may not have created a Brightcove Player, but still need your policy key (such as developing strictly with the Brightcove Native Player SDKs). In this case you will need to use the Policy API to get your policy key.
