The New Brightcove Player for Video Cloud

Now Video Cloud users can use the new Brightcove Player with their existing media assets. The Brightcove Player has many great new features you can deploy today, either alongside or replacing your existing Smart Players. The Brightcove Player is a new player based on new technology, so many of the customizations or plugins you may have relied on with the Smart Player may work differently or have to be reimplemented. Some features have been replaced or changed in important ways. Assembled here are materials and links that will help you understand the benefits of using the new Brightcove Player and what you should consider as you try it out.
