Transcode Settings

In this topic you will learn about recommended transcode settings for the new Brightcove Player.

Because the Brightcove Player will display HLS renditions whenever possible, we recommend that you alter your transcode settings to create a more robust set of HLS renditions. Transcode settings can be modified in your Account Settings. Note that to access the Account Settings, you must be a Video Cloud Studio user with the Administrator role.

To access the Account Settings:

  1. Login to Video Cloud Studio.
  2. On the home page, click the gear icon in the page header and then click Settings.

The Account Settings page will display.

Click on Transcode Settings to access and modify your transcode settings.

Note: when you modify transcode settings, the new renditions will be created only for new videos that you ingest. To get these renditions for existing videos, you will need to re-transcode them.
