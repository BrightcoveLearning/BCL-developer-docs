Getting Started with the Media Module

This topic provides an overview of the Media module which is used to manage videos in a Video Cloud account.

Note: The New Video Cloud Studio is currently in beta and is not supported by Brightcove Support. If you would like access to the beta, please contact your Brightcove Account Manager.

This topic contains the following sections:

  • Accessing the Media module
  • Accessing the side navigation panel
  • Using quick view
  • Editing video properties
  • Deleting videos
  • Adding videos to folders
  • Adding videos to playlists
  • Publishing videos and playlists

Accessing the Media module

To access the Media module, login to Video Cloud Studio and click Media in the navigation header.

The Media module can also be accessed at: https://studio.brightcove.com/products/videocloud-pre-release/media

The body of the page will display the following:

  • Video thumbnail and duration
  • Video name and ID
  • Last updated date and time
  • Reference Id
  • Status
    • - indicates the video is active
    • - indicates the video is inactive and can't be played

The video thumbnail also indicates whether the video is shared into the account, or shared out to another account. In the example below, the Tiger video is shared out to another account and the Great Horned Owl video was shared into this account.

Accessing the side navigation panel

The side navigation panel displays video, folder and playlist information and is displayed by default. The side navigation button can be used to hide and show the side navigation panel.

Using quick view

The Media module offers a quick view feature that can be used to quickly view metadata associated with a video. Access quick view by:

  • clicking on the the video thumbnail
  • double-clicking on the row with the video

The video list will expand to display the following:

  • Preview player
  • Short description
  • Video tags
  • Scheduling information
  • Publish link to publish the video
  • Delete link to delete the video

Double-click the row to close the quick view details.

Known Issue: Since the preview player is being loaded over HTTPS within the Studio, if your content is being served over HTTP, the preview player will not playback the content and your browser will display an insecure scripts message. We are working on a fix to address this issue and will update beta testers on the progress.

Deleting videos

To delete a video:

  • Click the checkbox associated with the video and then click Delete
  • Open quick view by clicking the video thumbnail or double-clicking the row the video is on and then click the Delete link

Confirm the delete by clicking the Delete button.
