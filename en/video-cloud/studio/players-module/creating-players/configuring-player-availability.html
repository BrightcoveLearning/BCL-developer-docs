Configuring Player Availability

This topic provides an overview of how to restrict the videos within a player to show only in specified domains.

Domain restrictions can be set on players to limit the domains where players can be used. If the player publishing code is copied and used on another site, domain restrictions would prevent the player from loading any videos.

Note: While domain restrictions can help prevent unauthorized distribution of your content, customers seeking maximum content security should look at DRM solutions instead. See Content Protection and Digital Rights Management for more information.

To configure a domain restriction for a player, follow these steps:

  1. Click Players in the navigation header to open the Players module.
  2. Click the link for the player you want to configure.
  3. Locate the Availability section and click Edit.
  4. Enter one or more complete URLs (such as http://brightcove.com) to restrict your player's availability to these particular domains. Leave out the "www" in the URL for more flexible restriction. Be sure to include either http:// or https://.

    Note: Complete URLs must be provided. Wildcards in URLs are not supported.

  5. Click Save to save the changes.

Note: Any changes made to a player may take up to 5 minutes to appear in published players.
