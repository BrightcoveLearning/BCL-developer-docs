Configuring the Player Endscreen

This topic covers how to configure the player endscreen.

Players can be configured so that an endscreen will be displayed when a video ends. The endscreen can be configured to show the replay and sharing icons or can be customized to include HTML. A custom endscreen is shown below.

Icons are displayed to allow the viewer to replay the video or share the video out to popular social media sites.

To configure a player endscreen, follow these steps:

  1. Click Players in the navigation header to open the Players module.
  2. Click the link for the player you want to configure.
  3. Locate the Endscreen section and click Edit.
  4. Select the Endscreen option. The following options are available:
    • Disabled - no endscreen will be displayed
    • Social Sharing - Display the social sharing dialog when video ends
    • Custom - Display a custom endscreen when video ends

Note: Any changes made to a player may take up to 5 minutes to appear in published players.

Social Sharing endscreen

When the Social Sharing option is selected, the social sharing dialog will display when the video ends. Note that social sharing must be enabled for the player to use the social sharing endscreen. If social sharing is not enabled, you will be prompted to enable social sharing when this option is selected. The social sharing options that are displayed are based upon what was configured as part of the social settings. For more information on social settings, see Configuring the Social Sharing Properties for a Player.

Custom endscreen

When the Custom option is selected, custom HTML can be used to display additional text or images above the replay and sharing icons. To configure a custom endscreen, select Custom and paste in HTML to be displayed in the Content property.
